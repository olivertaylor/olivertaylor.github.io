Oliver’s Favorite L.A. Restuarants

Here in Los Angeles you can get some of the best food in the world, and no matter where you are in the city there are thousands of wonderful restaurants near you. This guide is a list of my favorite places (I’ve been to all of them), organized by neighborhood.

If you might need it, and where available, get a reservation. This is L.A.
